WhatsApp Ordering Solutions for Restaurants

LIGOTECH LTD provides technology that lets restaurant customers place food orders directly through WhatsApp using AI-powered conversation.

About Us

LIGOTECH LTD is a UK-based technology company registered in England and Wales. We build WhatsApp-based ordering systems that allow restaurants and food businesses to receive orders from customers through WhatsApp, powered by artificial intelligence.

Our platform connects restaurant owners with their customers over WhatsApp Business, enabling a seamless ordering experience without the need for a separate app or website.

Our Services

We provide technology services to food businesses including:

🍽️

WhatsApp Food Ordering

Customers browse the menu and place orders through a natural WhatsApp conversation.

🤖

AI Order Assistant

An intelligent assistant that understands customer requests, manages carts, and confirms orders.

📦

Order Management

Restaurant owners receive instant notifications for every new order via email and WhatsApp.

🔌

Restaurant Onboarding

Simple setup for restaurants to connect their WhatsApp Business number and upload their menu.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

LIGOTECH LTD ("we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England and Wales. Registered address: 21 Alma Road, Sale, M33 4HE, United Kingdom. Contact: investoronkar@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

When customers use our WhatsApp ordering service, we collect:

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Display name (as provided by WhatsApp)
  • Order history (items ordered, quantities, prices)
  • Conversation messages related to placing orders

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfil food orders
  • To send order confirmations and status updates via WhatsApp
  • To personalise your ordering experience (e.g. remembering past orders)
  • To notify restaurant owners of new orders

4. Data Sharing

We share your order details with the restaurant you are ordering from. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) — for message delivery
  • OpenAI — for AI-powered order assistance
  • Cloud hosting providers — for data storage and processing

5. Data Retention

Order data is retained for up to 2 years for business record-keeping purposes. Conversation session data is deleted after 30 minutes of inactivity.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at investoronkar@gmail.com.
